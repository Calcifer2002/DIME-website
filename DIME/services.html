Our Services

Events

The “events” feature offers the user a list of organized events that are taking place in a set area within the coming weeks and gives the user the ability to purchase a ticket if required.

Reach Out

This feature was created to connect users who may be suffering from mental health issues with people who may have experience or advice. As mental health is a rising issue in our generation we aimed to make this experience as seamless and as friendly as possible

Chat

The “Chat” feature gives users a place to socialize and communicate in groups themed around certain topics such as computer games or sports. Anyone can join any group or users can interact with each other in private messages

Music

The “Music” feature analyses every user's music interest and matches them with songs that are trending with users with similar interests, it also offers a “listen party” feature where users can join in music streams and add songs to the playlist to enjoy with others
