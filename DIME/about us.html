About Us

We are an exceptional team of six students who have come together as one and discovered TeenHub. We all know someone that is new to the country and are trying to make friends but are struggling to. So we decided to make an app for students that struggling to make friends, an app where they can get to know other people just like them and make friends. With this app, our main focus was to help them with any mental health issues they might face with a change of scenery. This app is for their betterment and making sure they see Ireland as their home.
