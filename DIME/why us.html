Why TeenHub

As teenagers, we have seen others who have experienced isolation or have had mental health issues before and it might’ve been caused by different reasons such as moving to a new city or being an immigrant. Depression is a growing problem in Ireland, about 10,000 adolescents are facing it as we speak.

Teen Hub is a social media platform that allows teenagers to meet like minded people or attend events hosted nearby, the aim of this app is to help people get out from their comfort zone and make new friends and offer them the chance to discover new hobbies

Another study found that nearly 10,000 adolescents are struggling with mental health issues, who can be seen as potential customers in Ireland. With our plans to expand our market and target in European countries, we can increase our sales by around 20% according to the World Health Organisation, as mentioned 25% of the European population is affected by Depression.
